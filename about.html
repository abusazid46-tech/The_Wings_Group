Who We Are

The Story Behind Wings Group

Wings has become one of the most trusted e-commerce names in Northeast India. Under the leadership of CEO Prasanta Deb — MBA in Retail and HR with over 10 years of corporate experience — the group is deeply focused on uncompromising customer service.

Being a dynamic and diversified business group, Wings is dedicated to its vision of "all the good things in life should be accessible for everyone" — making life better for Indian consumers by delivering quality without compromise.

Northeast families turn to a wide range of home care services, security services, and facility management — all under a single Wing. With hi-tech commercial chemicals and world-class facilities, Wings products and services meet the highest industry standards.

Our team is trained, disciplined, and committed to delivering high-quality service using modern tools and efficient systems.
